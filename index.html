How to Use

  • Controls:

  • Drag & Drop: Drag an image into the center area to load it.
  • Move Image: Click and drag the image, use arrow keys, or arrow buttons to reposition.
  • Enlarge: Hold + button, press + key, scroll up, or use Enlarge button to increase size.
  • Shrink: Hold - button, press - key, scroll down, or use Shrink button to decrease size.
  • Flip: Press F key to mirror the image horizontally.
  • Rotate: Press R key to rotate the image clockwise.
  • Quit: Click Quit to exit the image and restart the page.

  • Filters:

  • Reset: Click Reset to recenter image and return to original size.
  • Invert: Click Invert to apply a reverse color effect to the image.
  • Grayscale: Click Grayscale to apply a grayscale effect to the image.
  • Dark Edges: Click Dark Edges to apply a filter to darken the image from the edges to the center.
  • Beige: Click Beige to apply a beige tone filter to the image.
  • Polarization: Click Polarization to removes glare, suppresses reflections from non-metallic surfaces, and increases color saturation
  • Acrylic: Click Acrylic to give an acrylic like apearance to the image
  • Median: Click Median to reduce noise specifically a "salt and pepper" effect on your image
  • Sobel: Click Sobel to apply a filter to outline edges on the image highlighting regions of high contrast

  • Transformation Filters:

  • Height: Create a hight map with the inputed image as a base
  • Normal: Create a normal map with the input image as a base

  • Channels:

  • Red: Use the Red slider to modify the red channel of the image
  • Green: Use the Green slider to modify the green channel of the image
  • Blue: Use the Blue slider to modify the blue channel of the image
  • Alpha: Use the Alpha slider to modify the alpha value of the image
  • Brightness: Use the Brightness slider to modify the brightness of the image
